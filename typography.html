Site in Need

Nepal is a developing country with almost 30 million population. Most of them live along the countryside and sizable numbers of people belong to the middle class family. People who come from middle class families want their children to have a prosperous life and good education. This is one of the many reasons that people in towns and cities are increasing day by day.And many things are done by PC Ultimately, demand for PCs is extremely high and more advanced computers need to be put in place.

We aim to Help

The main aim of this project work is to design and develop a functional Online PC purchasing Hub management platform which helps both owners and users in every possible ways.

Objectives

The following are the project objectives:
1. To develop an app that allows users to search, filter, and get their desired pc for using
2. To develop a system that allows the owner to add, edit, search and delete data from the database.
3. To develop a rental PC management system that allows the Owner to view user data as well as their PCâ€™s status
4. To help users to pay their invoice using electronic payment gateways. 5. To study and analyses the requirement specifications of the Online PC purchasing Hub
6. To apply google maps and manage shop location.
7. To give users the ability to interact, negotiate and pay with this application.

Difficulties

These are the question raised in this research:
1. What are the problems and opportunities of buying a PC from the owner we met online?How can we verify the validity of the owner and user?
2. How do we handle financial automation in our app?
3. What is the impact on pc buyers in nepal traffic is significantly high and low?
4. What are the technologies we are going to use to develop this app?
5. What can be the terms and conditions to be followed by owners and users.
6. How to maintain data integrity and security of the app?
7. Is it possible to make an Online PC purchasing hub in the context of Nepal.
